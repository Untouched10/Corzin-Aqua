Hard Water Problems in Indian Homes

If you see white stains, feel dry skin, or your geyser needs frequent repairs, hard water is usually the reason.

Hair Fall & Dry Skin

Hard water strips natural oils, leading to hair fall, dandruff, and rough skin after every shower.

White Stains on Taps

Stubborn calcium deposits ruin the look of bathroom fittings, tiles, and glass partitions.

Geyser Coil Burnout

Scale acts as an insulator, increases electricity bills, and damages heating coils over time.

Appliance Damage

Hardness reduces the lifespan of washing machines, dishwashers, and RO pre-filters.

Blocked Pipelines

Scale buildup narrows pipes, reduces pressure, and leads to expensive plumbing repairs.

High Soap Usage

Hard water prevents lather, forcing you to use more soap, shampoo, and detergent.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for borewell, tanker, and municipal water users.

Technology Does it remove TDS from water?
No. Corzin is a salt-free conditioner, not an RO. It reduces hardness minerals’ tendency to form scale, but retains minerals and avoids water wastage.
Water Source Is it suitable for borewell water?
Yes. Corzin is designed for high-hardness borewell and tanker water. A free water test helps choose the right model for your hardness level.
Maintenance How often do I need to change the media?
Typically 3–5 years depending on usage and hardness. No monthly salt refilling.
Technology Does it require electricity?
No. It works without electricity, so it also works during power cuts.
Maintenance Does it need salt refilling?
Never. That’s one of the biggest advantages over traditional salt-based softeners.
Usage Does it work for the entire house?
Yes. Installed at the overhead tank (or main inlet), it conditions water for all taps, showers, and appliances.
Service What is the warranty?
2-year warranty on the unit, with local support in Maharashtra.
